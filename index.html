Who Are We?

Verbum Networks in partnership with CIDJAP is an Information technology company and an Internet Service Provider (ISP) licensed by the Nigerian Communication Commission with the head office in Dominican Institute, Ibadan, South-West Nigeria. It began its operation here in Enugu on March 1st, 2005 at Catholic Institute for Development Justice and Peace (CIDJAP). As a corporate organization, Verbum Networks believes in providing affordable and innovative services that create an innovative edge for people. At Verbum Networks we constantly embark on research and development initiatives to add value to the services we provide as well as meet the ever-growing needs of our clients.
